How to get your cookies:

  1. Log in to Midasbuy
  2. Open your browser's developer tools (F12 or right-click > Inspect)
  3. Go to the "Application" or "Storage" tab
  4. Under "Cookies", select "https://www.midasbuy.com"
  5. Right-click and select "Copy all" or manually copy all cookies
  6. Paste the cookies in the text area below
